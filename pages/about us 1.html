About Us

DEPOT Software helps ISO Tank Container Service Providers streamline depot processes for greater efficiency.

As part of the Lucrosaft ICT Group, we are a leading provider of Depot Management Software for the ISO Tank Container industry. Based near the Port of Rotterdam, we’ve been innovating since 1986, ensuring safe, accessible, and eco-friendly depot environments. We’re passionate about staying ahead of industry trends and continuously improving to deliver the best solutions.
